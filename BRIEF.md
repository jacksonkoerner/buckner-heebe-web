# Buckner Heebe Tennis — Website Brief

## Overview
Build a clean, modern, single-page marketing website for **Buckner Heebe**, a tennis coach in the New Orleans metro area. This is a personal coaching brand site — sharp, professional, not bloated. Think modern sports coach landing page.

## About Buckner
- **Full Name:** Buckner Heebe
- **Current Role:** Head Tennis Coach (Boys & Girls) at Metairie Park Country Day School (MPCDS), Metairie, LA
- **Background:** MPCDS Class of 2019 alumnus. Multi-sport athlete — played football and tennis at Country Day. Returned to his alma mater as head coach.
- **Assistant Coach:** Chep Morrison
- **Team Achievements Under His Coaching:**
  - Boys: 1st at Regionals 2024, 3rd at State, all players qualified for State Championships
  - Girls: 2nd at Regionals 2023 & 2024, all players qualified for State 2024
  - Girls doubles team reached State semi-finals
  - Program has 18 combined state championships historically (11 boys, 7 girls)
- **Coaching Style:** Young, energetic coach who connects well with student-athletes. Fostering a culture of excellence, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Leading a program resurgence.

## Website Sections (Single Page)
1. **Hero Section** — Full-width hero with a coaching photo as background (use buckner-coaching-1.jpg or buckner-coaching-2.jpg). Bold name, tagline like "Tennis Coach | New Orleans" or similar. Clean CTA button to contact/book.

2. **About Section** — Brief bio covering his background: MPCDS alum, multi-sport athlete, returned as head coach. Keep it personable and professional. Mention his connection to the school and the program's rich history.

3. **Coaching Section** — What he offers:
   - Private Lessons (1-on-1 instruction for all levels)
   - Group Clinics (small group training)
   - High School Program (Head Coach at MPCDS)
   - Junior Development
   Each with a brief description. Use card-style layout.

4. **Results/Achievements Section** — Highlight the team's recent success and the program's championship legacy. Can use some stats or milestone numbers.

5. **Photo Gallery** — Display all 4 photos. The two Buckner coaching photos first, then the two player action shots. Simple grid or masonry layout.

6. **Contact Section** — Simple contact form (name, email, message) or at minimum a CTA with email/phone placeholder. Include a "Book a Lesson" prompt.

7. **Footer** — Name, location (New Orleans, LA), social links placeholders, copyright.

## Design Direction
- **Color Palette:** Navy blue (#1a237e or similar), tennis court green (#2e7d32 or similar), white, light gray backgrounds. Pull from the blue in his coaching shirt.
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif. Google Fonts — something like Inter, Montserrat, or similar modern font.
- **Style:** Modern, minimal, sporty. Think premium coaching brand, not a school website. Smooth scroll. Subtle animations on scroll (fade-in sections). Responsive — must look great on mobile.
- **Vibe:** Professional but approachable. He's a young coach, so don't make it stuffy.

## Technical Requirements
- **Pure HTML/CSS/JS** — no frameworks, no build tools. Single `index.html` file is fine (or index.html + style.css + script.js).
- **Responsive** — mobile-first design
- **Images** — reference from `images/` folder: `buckner-coaching-1.jpg`, `buckner-coaching-2.jpg`, `player-forehand.jpg`, `player-serve.jpg`
- **GitHub Pages ready** — just static files, no server needed
- **Performance** — lazy load images, keep it fast

## What NOT to do
- No cookie-cutter template feel
- No excessive animations or parallax
- No Lorem Ipsum — use real content based on the info above
- No heavy frameworks (React, etc.)
